Blueberry Muffin Recipe

Bake the ultimate blueberry muffins for your guests and loved ones. This recipe shows you how to create cafe-style blueberry muffins in your own kitchen.

Ingredients:

  • 100g fresh blueberries
  • 300g flour
  • 150g granulated sugar
  • 1 tsp. vanilla
  • 60 ml vegetable oil
  • 50g of butter
